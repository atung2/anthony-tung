Autoimmune Connect

Balancing Stakeholder Needs for an Consulting Service

Role:

Lead UX Researcher

Product Designer

Timeline:

Sep 2020 - Nov 2020

Skills:

Product Design

UX Research

Client:

SoundCloud

Problem:

DrBonnie360 is a company with two missions: 1) to provide consulting services to autoimmune-specific digital health agencies and 2) to provide educational and health-empowering resources to patients with autoimmune disease. Over the years, the company’s image has gradually shifted over from its consulting services to its patient advocacy. Because of the dual mission it tries to convey, the interface has complex taxonomy and an unclear user-flow that contributes to major usability issues.

Solution:

TThe goal of this project was to redesign the company’s interface to emphasize its consulting services while keeping patient resources easily accessible. This included both rebranding work (an in-depth case study of the company's new style guide soon to come) and restructuring information architecture to better direct users to the relevant pages. Because of DrBonnie360’s dual mission and services, a UX rework was especially important in order for both audiences to navigate to resources relevant to their experience.

  1. Provide multiple, personalized ways to discover music
  2. Minimize clutter and maximize efficiency
  3. Reorganize information to better fit user expectations
