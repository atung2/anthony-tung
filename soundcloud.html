SoundCloud

Music Curation for an Individualized Discovery Experience

Role:

Lead UX Researcher

Product Designer

Timeline:

Sep 2020 - Nov 2020

Skills:

Product Design

UX Research

Client:

SoundCloud

Problem:

SoundCloud has long been recognized as a platform for both sharing and discovery. Its uniqueness lies in the diversity of creators it attracts; from globally-recognized artists to small-town DJs, the range of people releasing content on SoundCloud justifies the staggering 125 million songs that make up its library. However, from our preliminary research, we found that despite their access to such a large variety of songs, SoundCloud listeners have difficulty discovering new music and navigating the app.

Solution:

The goal of this project was to identify and address the specific pain-points of SoundCloud listeners through a goal-oriented design workflow. As mentioned previously, preliminary research demonstrated that users were having difficulty discovering new music and navigating the app; as such, we aimed to create an intuitive interface that would emphasize music discovery. Our specific objectives were to:

  1. Provide multiple, personalized ways to discover music
  2. Minimize clutter and maximize efficiency
  3. Reorganize information to better fit user expectations
